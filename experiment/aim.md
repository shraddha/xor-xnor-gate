This experiment aims to:
1. Design and simulate transistor-level CMOS XOR and XNOR gate circuits using SPICE
2. Understand the behavior of NMOS and PMOS transistors in complex gate configurations
3. Analyze the voltage transfer characteristics (VTC) of CMOS XOR and XNOR gates
4. Learn to create and analyze SPICE netlists for XOR and XNOR gates
